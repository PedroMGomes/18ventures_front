About

This project was created to help the best founders execute their ideas without making mistakes. To have a trusted advisor that can make your project reach the next level. To be an early stage anger partner and investor.

We help companies before they fail.

Why does the company exist

Our focus is to bring out the best in founders, by allowing them to zero in on what they are great at. Taking the pressure off them by not having to work on tasks they are either not great at or simply don’t want to do but know they are important. 18Ventures specialises in supporting it’s portfolio companies with strategic expansion, business development and go-to-market launch.

Thesis

Invest in founders with relentless drive, a passion for what they do and a determination to solve problems.
